Welcome to, Prog Theta

Prog Theta is a dynamic and forward-thinking startup that is committed to promoting the use of Open Source software and providing opportunities for individuals to learn and develop and contribute to their coding skills. One of the most impressive initiatives of Prog Theta is the work they have done in teaching coding to over kids school kids. In collaboration with BBC's micro::bit program in Habiganj Zilla Public Library, Prog Theta has been able to introduce the basics of coding to children in a fun and engaging way.
